About

The Bicycle Coalition of Greater Philadelphia, SEPTA, NJ Transit, PATCO, DVRPC, and Open Plans are collaborating to determine how transit stations can better accommodate bicyclists. This map will help identify where the demand for better bicycle facilities is greatest.

Between September 21st and December 21st, 2012, the public could click on a station where they would like to see improvements, and respond to the questions in our survey. They could choose a station they bike to, or one they would bike to if improvements were made. Click the stations to see the input that we collected.

OpenPlans developed this map using its Shareabouts software, which is a tool to crowdsource data in a neighborhood or city. If you have any questions or comments, please contact us at bike@bicyclecoalition.org.
