Instructions

The Sister Cities Park Survey is a tool for collecting valuable metrics about park usage. It's really easy to get started.

Collect data consistently

Consistent data collection is important to creating useful data. Visit the park hourly and record the current usage of each section of the park at that moment. This will give you usage data (how many) that is also spatial (where) and temporal (when).

Download and explore your data

At any time you can click Download Data link to get a CSV file (a spreadsheet) of your data. Use this information to answer interesting questions like:
  • What day of the week is most popular?
  • What section of the park is most underutilized?
  • What is the average usage of each section?
The answers to these questions and more can provide you with useful insights to make improvements and expand successes for a better park for everyone.
