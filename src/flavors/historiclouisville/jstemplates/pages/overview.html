About

The University of Louisville's Urban Design Studio and the non-profit City Collaborative are passionate about Louisville's historic structures and spaces. We believe that our city's cultural and economic success in recent years has been greatly bolstered by our large concentrations of architectural treasures.

Add historic buildings, places and heritage resources in Louisville. Share a personal memory about why this site is important to you.

For over 40 years the historic preservation movement in Louisville has helped create a city with a unique flavor that is increasingly being recognized as an important cultural center and as a place with a high quality of life thanks to our historic urban neighborhoods.

As a city, we will use this mapping application to identify buildings, places, and heritage resources in Louisville that the community values as historic and worth saving and in the process initiating a necessary discussion about preservation.

The hope is that we can thoughtfully engage a large portion of Louisvillians by using this mapping application to identify the buildings, places, and spaces in Louisville that its community members value as historic and worthy of preservation.
