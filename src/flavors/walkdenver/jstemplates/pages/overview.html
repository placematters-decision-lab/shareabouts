Instructions

1. Move the map to the area you'd like to tell us about or use the crosshairs to automatically zoom to your current location.

2. Click on "Add a Place".

3. Drag the map until the pin is located where you want it.

4. Choose whether you want to:

  • record the number of pedestrians
  • record the street quality for walking
  • record the intersections quality for walking

5. Answer the questions. Click on Rating Tips in the menu for help deciding how to answer them.

6. Share with your friends by logging into twitter or facebook (top right of the menu).

Got it. Let's Get started!
